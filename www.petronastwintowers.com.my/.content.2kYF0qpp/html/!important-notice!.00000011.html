PETRONAS TWIN TOWERS, CLOSED TEMPORARILY FROM 18 MARCH 2020 UNTIL FURTHER NOTICE

The PETRONAS Twin Towers Visit Operations and the PETRONAS Twin Towers Gift Shop will continue to CLOSE TEMPORARILY until further notice. This is following the extension of the Movement Control Order (MCO) to 14 April 2020 as announced by the Prime Minister on 25 March 2020 to further mitigate the spread of COVID-19. We will review the COVID-19 situation on a daily basis and shall provide further updates on the re-opening date of the PETRONAS Twin Towers Visit Operations and Gift Shop in due time.

For visitors who have pre-purchased admission tickets, you may request to change your visit date or request for a refund by contacting us via email at . If the admission tickets were purchased via travel agents or applications such as Traveloka and Klook, kindly contact their respective management for the change of visit date or refund.

During the closure period, the PETRONAS Twin Towers Visit Operations and Gift Shop will continue to ensure that stringent measures on our visit areas and facilities are adhered to.

We apologise for any inconvenience caused and we look forward to welcoming visitors back to our premises soon.

Thank you.



Keep Calm and Stay Safe!
