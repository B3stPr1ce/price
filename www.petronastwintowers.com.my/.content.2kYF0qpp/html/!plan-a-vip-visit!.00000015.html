Enjoy special privileges exclusive for VIP visitors

Special guests are more than welcome on a private tour to the world’s one and only PETRONAS Twin Towers.

Why plan a VIP visit to PETRONAS Twin Towers?

Enjoy a briefing on the PETRONAS Twin Towers conducted by skilled and knowledgeable staffs along with a guided tour to the Skybridge on the 42nd Floor and the Observation Deck on the 86th Floor.

On top of that, special arrangements for VVIP Parking and dedicated lift services throughout the visit will also be arranged and made available to make the pleasant experience the most memorable one.

Group size Our team can arrange for VIP visits to accommodate a minimum amount of 2 pax with no maximum amount of pax in the VIP guest list.
Admission rate All VIP guests of PETRONAS Twin Towers enjoy an exclusive premium rate of RM127 per pax which is inclusive of private tour guides. Please contact us via the details provided below this page to liaise with the person-in-charge.
Payment & Confirmation The full payment by Credit Card (Amex, Visa, Mastercard or Alipay) or Cash must be made at the ticketing counter upon arrival. We will follow-up upon receiving the confirmation of your visit. Please refer to our Terms & Conditions for more information.
What to expect A 45-minute guided tour around the PETRONAS Twin Towers - Entry at Concourse -> Skybridge at Level 42 -> Observation Deck at Level 86 & Level 83.
Write to us To ensure that your booking process is a smooth sailing one, please provide the following details in your request letter:

1. Contact Person and Contact Number
2. Name(s) of VIP(s)
3. Date of Visit and Preferred Entry Time
4. Total Number of Visitors

We advise that you send in the request at least 7 days in advance prior to the day of visit.
Dress code requirements Kindly note that jeans, sandals, and round neck T-Shirts are not allowed.

Contact us now to book a slot for your visit

Kindly note that the earlier we are notified, the more time we have on hand in allocating our resources to ensure that your visit to the PETRONAS Twin Towers will be the most pleasant and memorable one yet.
