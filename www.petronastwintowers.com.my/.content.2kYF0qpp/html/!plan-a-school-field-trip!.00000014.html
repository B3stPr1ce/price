Learning can take place outside the classroom

There is only so much one can learn from textbooks. At PETRONAS Twin Towers, we guarantee a delightful experience and journey as we welcome students from all ages to set foot into Malaysia’s greatest masterpiece.

Gather the classroom outside and stand in awe at the foot of the twin towers, or gaze at the skyline from the top of the world - this school field trip is bound to inspire new ambitions in the hearts of today’s youths.

Things to explore in PETRONAS Twin Towers for school field trip

We offer a wide array of features to marvel at - inside out! Equipped with digital displays, tech-savvy youths can easily discover in-depth stories and histories about the twin towers more intimately through an interactive experience.

  • SkyBridge

    Measuring 170m (558 ft) above the ground and 58.4m (192 ft) long, the double-storey SkyBridge gives tourists a spectacular view of Kuala Lumpur city.

  • Observation Deck

    Located at Level 86 of both towers, the deck allows visitors to enjoy the view of the cityscape from above 370 meters.

  • Digital & Interactive Displays

    Touchscreen displays, holograms, projection mapping - we have a full-fledged digital facility to make every tourist’s experience as memorable as it can get.

  • Petrosains

    Experience science like never before! Dig deep into the fun and interactive world of the petroleum industry only at Petrosains, The Discovery Centre.
    For more information on Petrosains, go to https://petrosains.com.my/

Why plan a school field trip to PETRONAS Twin Towers?

The early bird gets the worm - when you plan ahead and book a slot for student groups, enjoy special discounts from PETRONAS Twin Towers as an incentive to encourage youths to visit one of the world’s greatest architectural wonders in Malaysia!

Group Size Groups of 20 - 40 pax may enjoy an exclusive School Group Discount at PETRONAS Twin Towers.
Admission Rate Registered government schools may enjoy a special entry rate. Please call our info booking line at +603-2331 8080 for more details.
Payment & Confirmation The full payment must be made at the ticketing counter upon arrival; no refunds will be entertained. Payment methods includes cash, Amex, Visa, Mastercard, and Alipay.

PETRONAS Twin Towers will follow-up upon receiving the confirmation of your visit. Please refer to our Terms & Conditions for more information.
What To Expect A 45-minute guided tour around the PETRONAS Twin Towers - Entry at Concourse -> Skybridge at Level 41 -> Observation Deck at Level 86 & Level 83.
Write To Us For a smoother booking process, please provide the following details in your request letter:

1. Contact Person and Contact Number
2. Name of Group
3. Date of Visit and Preferred Entry Time
4. No. of Visitors (Please specify Adult, Child or Infant)
